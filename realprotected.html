Welcome to the BloxyLeaks Information Page. Here, we provide an overview of the BloxyLeaks Staff Team.

About

The BloxyLeaks staff team is a diverse group comprising various departments, including moderation, administration, council, management, and ownership. The council department plays a pivotal role in overseeing and managing staff duties, ensuring efficiency and excellence.

Ranks

The BloxyLeaks Staff Team consists of the following ranks:

  • Moderation: Responsible for managing moderation on the server.
  • Administration: Comprising the server administrators.
  • Council Department: Oversees and manages the entire staff team.
  • Management: Responsible for server management.
  • Ownership: The owners of the server.
